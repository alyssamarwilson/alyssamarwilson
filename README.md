## Welcome

Tech can feel intimidating at first not because people are incapable of learning it, but because many concepts are introduced in ways that feel overly technical, disconnected from real life, or harder than they need to be.

This space is where I break down complex IT systems into approachable explanations, practical examples, visuals, and beginner-friendly PowerShell walkthroughs.
